In India, education and employment are often disconnected—students pursue degrees and wrong career paths, leading to unfulfilled dreams and unemployment. Lakshya Path bridges this gap by integrating career guidance, Vedic astrology, and job opportunities, ensuring rural students and parents make informed choices for a brighter future.

How This Works

At Lakshya Path, we follow a simple yet effective process to help you achieve your career goals while working to transform your area into an educational hub:

  • Discover Your Path: Share your birth details and interests to get a personalized career roadmap using Vedic astrology.
  • Explore Opportunities: Access our resources to learn about courses, skills, and job options that match your profile.
  • Build an Educational Hub: Share your area’s needs through our market research form—we’ll use this data to bring reputed coaching centers and job opportunities to your region, making it an educational hub for a better future.
  • Stay Supported: Connect with our counselors for ongoing guidance and join our community for mentorship and support.

Our Commitment: We’re trying to make your area an educational hub by bringing quality education and employment opportunities closer to you!
